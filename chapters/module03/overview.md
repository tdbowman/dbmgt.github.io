## Topics Covered

1. **ER Modeling Concepts**

   - Attributes (simple, composite, derived, multivalued)
   - Relationships

2. **ER Diagrams**
   - Crow's foot notation
   - Cardinality (1:1, 1:N, M:N)
   - Participation (total vs partial)
