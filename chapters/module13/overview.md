# Module 13: Business Intelligence

## Overview

How do organizations turn data into insights? This week explores **Business Intelligence (BI)**, data warehousing, and analytics.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

- Explain the purpose of data warehouses
- Describe ETL processes
- Compare OLAP and OLTP systems
- Discuss BI tools and reporting

## Topics Covered

1. **Data Warehousing**
   - Data warehouse vs operational database
   - Star and snowflake schemas

2. **Business Intelligence**
   - ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)
   - OLAP vs OLTP
   - Reporting and dashboards
